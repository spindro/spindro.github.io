I am an assistant professor (rtd-A) at Sapienza University of Rome interested in trustworthy machine learning. I am a member of the PINlab led by prof. Galasso on a mission to develop interpretable perception algorithms that leverage the uncertainty inherent in deep learning models. I am also into algorithmic fairness, particularly in alternative learning strategies to comprehend and mitigate the bias in the data. I hold a PhD in ICT, advised by Prof. Scardapane on graph neural networks. Feel free to contact me if you are looking for research collaborations or thesis supervision.
